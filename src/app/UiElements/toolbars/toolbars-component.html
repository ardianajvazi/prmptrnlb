Toolbars

Toolbar actions appear above the view affected by their actions.

Usage

Toolbars appear a step above the content they affect. They may constrain their width to accommodate material passing over them. Do not separate a toolbarâ€™s actions by another sheet of material, unless it is transient, such as a menu or dialog.

Toolbars appear a step above the sheet of material affected by their actions. When sheets scroll underneath toolbars, they are clipped and cannot pass through to the opposite side.
